Tips for Success
⏱
Guard the Clock
2 hours of floor time goes fast with 16 directors. Designate a timekeeper and give groups a 15-min warning before regrouping.
📊
Lead with Data
Have your stats printed or on a device. Directors appreciate specifics — weekly return volume, QC rates, production throughput.
🚗
New Car as Flex Asset
If groups get uneven or a director wants to see a different area, use the new car to move people without disrupting the flow.
💧
Hospitality Details Matter
Water on the lot signals you thought ahead. Small things like this set a professional tone and show operational awareness.
📱
Tablet Readiness
Charge the night before. Confirm login credentials work. A tablet that won't log in during a director visit is a distraction you don't want.
🎯
Strong Debrief Close
End with confidence. A 1–2 sentence summary of what they saw and one thing you're proud of leaves a lasting impression before the shuttle.
